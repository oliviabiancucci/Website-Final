Our Story

Cam cooking in his home.

Cam's Kitchen was established in 2018 by Cameron Fraser. He began his culinary journey by cooking for friends and family and he quickly gained recognition. He started catering for events when eventually he decided to open his own restaurant. Cam's kitchen is the place to be together and share in life's happy moments. We invite you to experience our exceptional place and look forward to serving you!

Opening day on April 11th, 2018.
